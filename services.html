What we provide

Our team at With Grace works relentlessly to ensure we provide a flawless service. Our organisation runs weekly markets throughout December, to ensure the produce used is at its peak quality. Many of our volunteers are locals from the community, driven by hope, they work alongside our team in order to provide high-quality comforting meals. Through simple acts of care, With Grace strengthens the community and makes sure no one feels left behind.


About Our Suppliers


With Grace

Our menu is built on the exceptional ingredients sourced from trusted Northern Irish producers, each bringing their own craft to our kitchen. We work closely with Season Harvest, whose vibrant vegetables—pulled from rich local soil—arrive to us at peak freshness. Our meats come from Lisdergan’s Butchers, renowned for their grass‑fed beef and lamb, raised with care on small farms that prioritise quality and flavour. From the coast, we proudly serve fish supplied by Donegal Prime, whose dedication to sustainable, cold‑water sourcing ensures every fillet carries the clean taste of the Atlantic. Together, these partnerships allow us to celebrate the very best of our region on every plate.

We ensure that the ingredients we use are the highest quality attainable.

Our roast beef is the centrepiece of comfort and craft, slow‑cooked to achieve a tender, blushing interior and a caramelised crust that speaks of patience and precision. Each slice reveals deep, savoury richness, complemented by its own natural juices reduced into a glossy, full‑bodied gravy. We serve it alongside seasonal vegetables roasted until golden, their sweetness balancing the beef’s robust flavour, and a Yorkshire pudding that rises light and crisp from the oven. It’s a dish that brings together tradition, warmth, and the unmistakable satisfaction of a perfectly cooked roast.

Our roast chicken is served golden and succulent, its skin crisped to perfection while the meat stays tender and full of flavour. It’s paired with Brussels sprouts roasted alongside smoky pancetta, allowing the edges to caramelise before being finished with a drizzle of balsamic vinegar for a bright, tangy lift. On the side, we add rich, indulgent Hasselback potatoes, slow‑roasted in goose fat until each delicate ridge turns crisp and buttery. Together, it’s a dish that balances comfort, elegance, and the kind of depth that only careful, attentive cooking can deliver.

Our roast cauliflower is gently braised in a rich blend of Japanese unagi and celeriac stock, allowing each floret to absorb layers of sweet, savoury umami before being finished to a golden roast. It’s served alongside a silky confit‑garlic mash, whipped until luxuriously smooth with a mellow, aromatic warmth. Crispy shallots add a delicate crunch, while a spoonful of black bean chilli rayu brings heat, depth, and a touch of smoky richness. Together, the dish offers a vibrant balance of texture and flavour, transforming humble vegetables into something quietly indulgent and deeply satisfying.
